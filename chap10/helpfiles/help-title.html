The Title field is used to enter the title of your recipe.

While the title you choose for your recipe should accurately represent the recipe, you should feel free to use langauge that differentiates your recipe for the many similar recipes that other memebers may be entering.

For example, rather than "French Onion Soup", choose a more colorful name such as "Uncle Fred's French Onion Soup with Herbed Croutons".

You may not leave this field blank.
